Our Story

We Believe in
Freedom by Design

Tiny Haven was born from a simple belief — that a beautiful, meaningful home doesn't have to cost you your life savings, your freedom, or your values.

We started in 2016 with one workshop, two builders, and a stubborn conviction that people deserved better. Eight years and 500+ homes later, we're still building with the same hands-on care and the same obsession with quality that started it all.

Every home we build is a conversation — between your vision and our craft. We don't do cookie-cutter. We don't do cheap. We do yours.
